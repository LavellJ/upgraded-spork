## 1. SCOPE AND DEFINITIONS

### 1.1 Scope
This Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") governs LearnOz's processing of Personal Data on behalf of School in connection with the LearnOz educational platform services ("Services").

### 1.2 Definitions
- **"Personal Data"**: Student educational records, teacher professional information, and related data as defined in applicable privacy laws
- **"Processing"**: Collection, use, storage, analysis, transmission, or other handling of Personal Data
- **"Data Subject"**: Students, teachers, and other individuals whose Personal Data is processed
- **"Supervisory Authority"**: Applicable data protection authority or educational privacy regulator

## 2. DATA PROCESSING DETAILS

### 2.1 Categories of Data Subjects
- **Students**: Enrolled learners using LearnOz platform (ages 5-18)
- **Teachers**: Licensed educators and classroom instructional staff
- **Parents/Guardians**: Legal representatives of minor students (communication only)
- **Administrators**: School officials with educational oversight responsibilities

### 2.2 Categories of Personal Data
- **Student Academic Data**: Learning progress, lesson completion, performance metrics, curriculum alignment
- **Behavioral Analytics**: Engagement patterns, time-on-task, help-seeking behavior, accessibility usage
- **Educational Content**: Student journal entries, reflection responses, assignment submissions
- **Professional Data**: Teacher assignments, professional development, instructional analytics
- **Technical Data**: System access logs, error reports, security monitoring (pseudonymized when possible)

### 2.3 Purposes of Processing
- **Educational Instruction**: Personalizing learning experiences and providing adaptive curriculum
- **Progress Monitoring**: Tracking academic growth and identifying intervention needs
- **Professional Support**: Supporting teacher professional development and instructional planning
- **System Operations**: Maintaining platform security, reliability, and technical functionality
- **Compliance Reporting**: Fulfilling legal obligations for educational record keeping

### 2.4 Retention Periods
- **Active Learning Data**: Duration of school enrollment plus 3 years
- **System Logs**: 90 days from creation
- **Optional Feedback**: 2 years or until consent withdrawal
- **Backup Data**: 1 year maximum retention

## 3. LEARNOZ OBLIGATIONS

### 3.1 Processing Instructions
LearnOz will:
- Process Personal Data only for purposes outlined in Section 2.3
- Follow School's documented instructions for data handling
- Not process Personal Data for LearnOz's own commercial purposes
- Implement technical and organizational measures to protect Personal Data

### 3.2 Confidentiality and Security
LearnOz will:
- Ensure authorized personnel are bound by confidentiality obligations
- Implement industry-standard security measures including encryption and access controls
- Conduct regular security assessments and vulnerability testing
- Notify School within 72 hours of any Personal Data breach

### 3.3 Subprocessors
LearnOz may engage subprocessors for:
- **Cloud Infrastructure**: [CLOUD PROVIDER NAME] for data hosting and backup
- **Security Services**: [SECURITY VENDOR NAME] for monitoring and threat detection
- **Support Services**: [SUPPORT VENDOR NAME] for customer service and technical assistance

LearnOz will:
- Maintain current list of subprocessors available to School
- Provide 30 days advance notice of any new subprocessors
- Ensure all subprocessors meet same data protection standards

### 3.4 Data Subject Rights Assistance
LearnOz will assist School in responding to:
- **Access Requests**: Providing complete exports of Personal Data
- **Rectification Requests**: Correcting inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Erasure Requests**: Securely deleting Personal Data when legally permissible
- **Portability Requests**: Exporting data in machine-readable formats
- **Objection Requests**: Restricting or stopping specific processing activities

**Response Time**: Within 30 days of School's request
**Format**: JSON/CSV export with complete audit trail

## 4. SCHOOL OBLIGATIONS

### 4.1 Lawful Basis and Authority
School represents that it:
- Has lawful authority to share Personal Data with LearnOz
- Has obtained necessary consents or established legal basis for processing
- Will comply with applicable privacy laws including FERPA, COPPA, and GDPR
- Will provide required privacy notices to Data Subjects

### 4.2 Data Accuracy and Updates
School will:
- Provide accurate and up-to-date Personal Data
- Promptly notify LearnOz of any data corrections needed
- Update student enrollment and teacher assignment information
- Remove access for departed students and staff

### 4.3 Privacy Rights Management
School will:
- Handle Data Subject privacy rights requests in first instance
- Forward technical requests to LearnOz within 5 business days
- Provide clear privacy information to students, parents, and teachers
- Maintain records of consent and data processing notices

## 5. CROSS-BORDER DATA TRANSFERS

### 5.1 Geographic Restrictions
- **Primary Processing**: EU/EEA regions for European students
- **US Processing**: FERPA-compliant infrastructure for US students  
- **Backup Locations**: Same or equivalent jurisdiction as primary processing
- **No Third-Country Transfers**: Without adequate protection measures

### 5.2 Transfer Safeguards
When transfers outside home jurisdiction are necessary:
- **Adequacy Decisions**: To countries approved by relevant data protection authorities
- **Standard Contractual Clauses**: EU-approved terms ensuring equivalent protection
- **Additional Safeguards**: Technical measures including encryption and access controls

## 6. AUDIT AND COMPLIANCE

### 6.1 Audit Rights
School may audit LearnOz's compliance through:
- **SOC 2 Type II Reports**: Annual third-party security and privacy audits
- **Questionnaire Assessments**: Annual completion of School's vendor assessment
- **On-Site Audits**: With 30 days advance notice and reasonable scope
- **Certification Reviews**: ISO 27001, privacy framework certifications

### 6.2 Documentation and Records
LearnOz will maintain:
- **Processing Records**: Detailed logs of data processing activities
- **Security Incident Reports**: Documentation of any security events
- **Subprocessor Agreements**: Contracts ensuring equivalent data protection
- **Training Records**: Evidence of staff privacy and security training

## 7. INCIDENT RESPONSE

### 7.1 Data Breach Notification
LearnOz will notify School within **72 hours** of becoming aware of any Personal Data breach involving:
- **Unauthorized Access**: Potential exposure of Personal Data to unauthorized persons
- **Data Loss**: Accidental deletion or corruption of Personal Data
- **System Compromise**: Security incidents affecting data integrity or availability

### 7.2 Incident Information
Breach notifications will include:
- **Nature of Breach**: Type of incident and data categories affected
- **Scope Assessment**: Number of Data Subjects and data records involved
- **Risk Evaluation**: Potential impact on Data Subjects' rights and freedoms
- **Mitigation Measures**: Steps taken to address breach and prevent recurrence

### 7.3 Cooperation
LearnOz will cooperate with School's incident response including:
- **Regulatory Notifications**: Assisting with required breach reports to authorities
- **Data Subject Notifications**: Supporting individual breach notifications if required
- **Investigation Support**: Providing logs and evidence for forensic analysis
- **Remediation Planning**: Implementing additional safeguards to prevent similar incidents

## 8. TERMINATION

### 8.1 Data Return and Deletion
Upon termination of Services, LearnOz will:
- **Export Option**: Provide complete export of Personal Data in portable format
- **Secure Deletion**: Delete all Personal Data within 90 days unless legally required to retain
- **Backup Purging**: Remove Personal Data from backup systems and archives
- **Certification**: Provide written confirmation of deletion completion

### 8.2 Transition Assistance
LearnOz will provide reasonable assistance with:
- **Data Migration**: Supporting transfer to new educational technology providers
- **Documentation**: Providing data maps and processing records for transition
- **Technical Support**: Assisting with export format compatibility
- **Timeline Coordination**: Working within School's transition schedule

## 9. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

### 9.1 Data Protection Liability
LearnOz will be liable for damages resulting from:
- **Processing Violations**: Failure to comply with this DPA or applicable data protection laws
- **Security Failures**: Inadequate security measures leading to Personal Data breach
- **Unauthorized Processing**: Processing Personal Data beyond scope of School's instructions

### 9.2 Limitation of Liability
LearnOz's liability is limited to:
- **Direct Damages**: Actual financial losses directly resulting from LearnOz's breach
- **Regulatory Fines**: LearnOz's proportionate share of fines imposed for joint violations
- **Cap**: Total liability not to exceed annual Service fees paid by School

This DPA may be amended:
- **By Mutual Agreement**: Written amendments signed by both parties
- **Legal Updates**: Updates required by changes in applicable privacy laws
- **Standard Clauses**: Updates to reflect current regulatory guidance
